🍋 LS Autofill Bookmarklet

One-click form-filler for https://app.lemonsqueezy.com/products/new. Zero install, runs in your own Chrome where the LS domain isn't blocked.

Install — pick one of two ways

Way A: Drag to bookmarks bar (easy)

  1. Show your browser's bookmarks bar (Ctrl+Shift+B)
  2. Drag this button onto it:

🍋 Fill Next LS Product

Way B: Copy-paste (if the drag glitches)

  1. Right-click your bookmarks bar → Add page… or Add bookmark
  2. Name: LS Autofill
  3. Paste the URL below into the URL field → Save

Use it

  1. Go to https://app.lemonsqueezy.com/products/new
  2. Click the 🍋 Fill Next LS Product bookmark
  3. A purple panel appears (top-right) showing the product name, price, and file path to upload
  4. Manually drag the .cs file onto LS's upload zone (file upload can't be automated from a bookmarklet — browser security)
  5. Toggle Generate license keys for paid products
  6. Click Publish
  7. Navigate back to /products/new, click bookmark again — auto-advances to product 2, 3, 4…
  8. Progress persists in your browser's localStorage — close and resume anytime
If a field doesn't auto-fill: LS sometimes changes their form DOM. The banner will say ✗ name field not found (or similar). Just fill that one field by hand — the rest still work. Tell me if one breaks and I'll adjust the selectors.
