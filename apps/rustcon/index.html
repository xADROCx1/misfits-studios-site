FAQ

stuff_admins_ask · before_install

Does RustCON need a server-side plugin?

No. RustCON connects over standard WebRcon, the same way the in-game F1 console and the official Facepunch tools do. If your server has RCON enabled, RustCON works.

Does it work with Oxide (uMod) and Carbon?

Yes — both. The permissions manager has native support for Oxide and Carbon group/user/permission models. Toggle between frameworks in the app.

How is RustCON different from Rust+?

Rust+ is the official companion app for players — map, base alerts, team chat. RustCON is the admin's panel — RCON console, player management, permissions, item giving, events, kits. Different tool, different audience.

What does it cost?

Free trial for new users. After the trial, an active subscription is required: $3.99/month or $29.99/year. No ads. No dark patterns. Cancel anytime through the App Store or Google Play.

Which platforms are supported?

Live now on the Apple App Store (iPhone, iPad, M1+ Macs, Vision Pro) and on Google Play (Android). Desktop builds (Windows, macOS, Linux) are in active development.

Is this affiliated with Facepunch Studios?

No. RustCON is an independent third-party admin tool that talks to the standard Rust RCON endpoint. Rust® is a trademark of Facepunch Studios.
