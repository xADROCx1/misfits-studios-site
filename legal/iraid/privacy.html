Privacy_Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-24 · Last updated: 2026-04-24

Applies to the IRAID mobile application ("IRAID", "the app") published by Shadow Kids Studios ("we", "us"). For the studio-wide website policy, see /privacy.html.

// tl;dr

IRAID does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you. We do not use accounts, ads, third-party analytics, or tracking SDKs. Your preferences (your loadout, target list, server preset) live only on your phone. The app fetches a public raid-data manifest URL from our servers; that request contains no user data. If you buy a subscription, our payment processor RevenueCat handles the transaction; see § 4.

1. What we collect from you

Nothing that identifies you. Specifically:

  • No account, no sign-up, no login. IRAID has no account system.
  • No personal information — no name, email, phone, address, location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access. The app does not request any runtime permissions for these.
  • No device advertising identifiers — we do not read IDFA (iOS) or GAID (Android).
  • No analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting in version 1.x. If we add crash reporting or opt-in analytics in a later version, this policy will be updated and the in-app disclosure will change before the feature ships.

2. What is stored on your device

IRAID stores your local preferences using Android SharedPreferences / iOS NSUserDefaults (via the platform's AsyncStorage API). These include:

  • Your chosen server preset (Vanilla, 2x, 5x, Primitive)
  • Your chosen loadout profile (Solo, Duo, Small Team, Zerg, Wipe Day)
  • The list of explosives you've marked as available
  • Your saved planner targets
  • The raid-data bundle cache (see § 3)

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app erases it.

3. What we transmit

One outbound request category:

  • At app startup, and at most once per 24 hours, IRAID makes an anonymous HTTPS GET to https://shadowkidsstudios.com/api/iraid/data/manifest.json and, if a newer data bundle is available, to the manifest's published URL. These requests contain no user data beyond the standard HTTP fields your device sends to any web server (IP address, User-Agent, TLS handshake).
  • Our server logs these requests for basic operational purposes (bandwidth monitoring, error detection). We do not attempt to identify individuals from these logs. Logs are kept for a maximum of 30 days and then rotated out.

No other network calls are made by the app in version 1.x.

4. Subscriptions and payments

IRAID offers optional monthly and annual subscriptions that unlock auto-updating raid data. If you purchase a subscription:

  • Your payment is handled entirely by Apple's App Store or Google Play, not by us. We never see or store your payment card, bank account, or billing address.
  • We use RevenueCat as our subscription-management provider. RevenueCat generates an anonymous app-user ID, records the transaction receipt for entitlement validation, and collects standard device metadata (device model, OS version, app version, country, and their own logs). RevenueCat's privacy policy governs their processing.
  • You can cancel a subscription at any time through the Google Play Store or Apple Subscriptions settings. Refunds are governed by the platform's policy.

5. Children

IRAID is intended for an adult audience (the game it is a calculator for is rated 16+). The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children. Because the app collects no personal information from anyone, we have no child-specific data to delete, but if you believe a child's data has somehow been collected, email us at misfits.support@proton.me and we will investigate.

6. Your rights (GDPR, CCPA, and similar)

Because we do not collect personally-identifying information, there is generally nothing for us to disclose, delete, or export on your behalf. You can exercise control by:

  • Clearing the app's storage via your phone's settings, which wipes all local preferences
  • Uninstalling the app, which removes everything
  • Cancelling your subscription through Google Play or Apple directly

If you have a specific data question, email misfits.support@proton.me and we will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

All network traffic is over HTTPS / TLS. Local preferences are stored via the platform's standard secure storage APIs. We do not run an account server, so there is no credential database to breach.

8. Changes to this policy

If we make a material change — for example, adding opt-in analytics or introducing any feature that transmits user-identifying data — we will update this page, change the "Last updated" date, and surface an in-app notice before the new behaviour ships. Non-material edits (typos, clarifications, broken link fixes) are logged silently.
