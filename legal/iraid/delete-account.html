For the IRAID mobile application. See also: Privacy Policy · Terms of Service.

// tl;dr

IRAID has no account system. There is nothing to "delete" in the traditional sense — there is no profile, no login, no server-side identity. Your in-app preferences live only on your phone. Uninstalling the app erases them. If you have ever subscribed to IRAID Pro, our payment processor (RevenueCat) holds an anonymous purchase record indexed by a randomly-generated app user ID. To remove that record, email us using the form below.

1. What gets deleted automatically when you uninstall

When you uninstall IRAID from your phone, Android (and iOS, if applicable) erase the app's local storage. That covers everything we keep on-device:

  • Your chosen server preset (Vanilla, 2x, 5x, Primitive)
  • Your chosen loadout profile (Solo, Duo, Small Team, Zerg)
  • Your list of available explosives
  • Your saved Planner targets
  • The cached raid-data bundle (if you're a Pro subscriber)

No copy of any of this data exists on our servers. Uninstalling the app is therefore a complete deletion of all on-device data IRAID ever stored about you.

2. What may still exist on third-party services

IRAID uses two third-party services. Neither receives any personally-identifying information from us, but they do retain functional records:

  • RevenueCat — if you ever bought (or trialled) IRAID Pro, RevenueCat holds an anonymous app user ID + purchase history (transaction date, product ID, subscription status). This record is indexed by a random ID, not by your name or email.
  • Google Play Billing / Apple App Store — your purchase history with the platform store itself is owned by Google or Apple, not by us. To delete it, use Google's or Apple's account-data tools. We can't delete records that belong to them.

Server-side request logs from the manifest fetch (the once-per-24h call described in the privacy policy § 3) are kept at most 30 days and rotate out automatically. They contain no IRAID-specific identifiers — just standard HTTP metadata that any web server records.

3. How to request RevenueCat record deletion

If you've ever subscribed to IRAID Pro and want the RevenueCat record removed, send us an email. We will forward the request to RevenueCat under their data-subject-rights process, which they fulfil within 30 days.

Sends an email to misfits.support@proton.me with a pre-filled template. You can also email us manually using the same address and the subject line "Delete IRAID data".

Step 2 — uninstall the app from your phone if you haven't already (this clears all on-device data immediately, regardless of when we process the email).

Step 3 — we respond within 30 days with confirmation. We do not store your email beyond what is needed to fulfil the request and confirm completion to you. The reply will be deleted from our support inbox within 90 days of resolution.

4. What we cannot delete for you

  • Active subscription billing. If you want to stop being billed, cancel inside Google Play Subscriptions (Android) or Apple Subscriptions (iOS). We cannot cancel a subscription on your behalf — those settings live in the platform store, not in our systems.
  • Your platform-store purchase history. Google and Apple maintain their own records of every purchase you make. To remove those, use Google's or Apple's account-deletion tools.
  • Reviews you may have left on Google Play / App Store. Use the platform's own review-management tools.

5. Verification

We do not require ID verification for IRAID data-deletion requests, because IRAID does not collect any identifying information that we could verify against. The email you send us is the only contact channel we have. To prevent malicious deletion of someone else's RevenueCat record, we may ask you to provide an order ID from your purchase confirmation email — that detail is something only the genuine subscriber would have.
